Let Us Tell You About Our Transition

VETROCORE International Limited was founded in 2018. Our founders saw the problems faced by startups, SMES, which prevents them from becoming scalable enterprises from lack of funding, expert operation, adequate business advisory, substantial support service and mostly bad and poor design of services, products and the business; hence we were formed as a technology company rendering enterprise architectural and design consulting services to organisations and businesses majorly SMES and Startups, utilising technology as a driving tool for the actualisation of the set objectives.

We take a wholistic view of your service(s), solution, product(s) and business and help you design and/or redesign it to give it a competitive advantage in our fast paced world where technology is a driving tool for evolvement and growth.

We have a pact team of Design Architects and Solution Design professionals who apply design thinking skills and design methodologies while working closely with our robust team of Product Managers, Business Analysts, Software Engineers and Enterprise Architects to help you launch and/or relaunch a bankable product and/or service.

The belief of our founder, is that; the private sector is the key enabler of economy and social wealth creation around the globe. Driven by this philosophy, we invest for the long-term, bringing sector expertise and operational excellence to companies we invest in.

Our relentless focus on enduring results and sustainable growth: We create bold, innovative solutions that work pragmatically for your whole organization.


Our committed, collaborative and transparent people: We care about you, your team and your business as if it were our own.


Our change proactiveness: We reject the status quo and seek to help you define a new future therefore we design for change.


Our attention to your journey as well as your destination: We focus on what matters the most, anticipating the risks, barriers and opportunities to make change stick.
